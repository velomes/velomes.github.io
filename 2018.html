What?

Velo□□mes is a webpage showing rider's scores according to so-called "velogames rules".

What about leagues?

Leagues are supported as well! Tweet with #velomes and your league id and it will be added. You can find the league id in the past races you've participated in this year.

Were you in one of the official leagues? You can see them here: Twitter and Facebook.

Can I help?

Sure! If you've found an error in the scoring please tweet with the abovementioned hashtag.
